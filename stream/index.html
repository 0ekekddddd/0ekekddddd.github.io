about the stream

"Quitter's Park" is a public chatroom & rave radio powered by twitch.tv.

I stream whenever I want. Bookmark this page and check in from 5pm - 4am CST or mornings if you want to catch me.

I like to listen to edm/idm from uk gabber to breakcore, so that's what we broadcast while I work on stuff.

I'm mute and choose to hang out amongst the chat. I have a pinhole-range of interests that I will deeply talk about and I take time to warm up to strangers, but as long as you're decent and sincere I'll kick back with anyone who drops in to chat.

Anything you want to know about me worth knowing, you have to be here to ask me about it.
