TEXAS KISS RHINESTONE にようこそ

An online lookbook and tribute to my boots and flashy rhinestones.

I'm a street gothic stylist with Harajuku & Western styling sensibilities.

I live my indulgences & luxury solo and stay honest to who I want to be. Now and then I take part in my city's EGL swapmeets and keep up with online forums, but I like to hang out at the park in my free time, do my daily checkup on the gachapon stores, and take pictures of food.

Street armor is important. Hopefully the way I wear myself tells you just the kind of person I am, and encourages you to take care of your clothes, your community, and your self.
