About The Webmaster

i'm tkr - web revival backer, ethical luddite, collapsnik...

vietnamese apathetic-androgyne residing in the states.

i do illustration & webdev, and most computertech.

i have a narrow interest in 2001-2004's retro consumer design which includes website ui (just look around lol). my portfolio takes after the newsprint-based design of japanese maximalist websites. think compact and information-heavy.

i am a neocities veteran and former moderator of some webmaster's forum before i decided to migrate hosts and change course of my online presence. i still keep in touch with my webmastery of course. whether its markdown or C, i don't think its ever too late to learn something new.

my illustrationwork is in the gorey and erotic. sometimes both.

i'm also into a lot of expensive hobbies. you'll easily find my many projects and collections that i've been documenting over the years.

About The Website

100% handedcoded by human

static and screenreader-accessible. written in notepad++. layout graphic sketched, finalized, and dismembered on gimp.

technical information

  1. hosted on github pages. all opinions represented across this URL are my own and do not reflect on any of the third parties used to make this site.
  2. minimal javascript. structural data is stored on a single script and cycles only on-site data. graphic uniform is stitched together with objects and doc.write (guarantee your personal information is not important to me whatsoever...)
  3. imagemaps and some screenreader-unfriendly elements are present.
